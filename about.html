About the People Awareness Organization

⭐This organization was started in 2022 to educate people about mental health struggles which face all of us, in hopes of helping people feel more supported and connected.

⭐If people are more knowledgeable about mental health, they can prevent mental health disorders within themselves, and know that they can get out of them if they are stuggling.
